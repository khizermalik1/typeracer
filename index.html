How to Use TypeRacer
  1. Select your desired difficulty level from the dropdown.
  2. A sample text will appear. You can refresh it for a new one of the same difficulty.
  3. Click in the typing area and begin typing. The timer will start automatically.
  4. As you type, correct words will be highlighted in blue, incorrect in red.
  5. Press Enter to finish the test. The typing area will be disabled and your results will be shown.
  6. Click Retry to try a new sample of the same difficulty. The typing area will be cleared and re-enabled.
  7. WPM is calculated based on the number of correct words and the time taken.

Tip: You can change the difficulty or refresh the sample at any time before starting a new test.
